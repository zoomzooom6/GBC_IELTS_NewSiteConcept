Registration Information

Step 1: Prepare your identity document

The only acceptable documents are a valid passport or Canadian Permanent Resident Card.

Please take a photo or scan your passport or PR Card and ensure it:
  • Is valid
  • Has your signature on it. Some passports have your signature on another page. Please also scan or take a picture of this page.
  • Has a clear photo
  • Will be the same identification you use on the test day.
  • All information is legible and not cut off in any way.
  • Is in colour
You MUST bring this document with you on the test day for identification purposes. Please do not send your passport away to obtain a travel visa or to be renewed.

Note: Failure to notify us of any Identification Document changes prior to your test will mean you will be unable to write the test and a forfeit of your test registration with no option for refund or test day transfer.


Step 2: Online Registration

Click on the link below to begin your registration.

Paper-based and computer-delivered tests are available.


You will need to upload a copy of your identification document to your test registration.


Step 3: Payment

The test fee is $325.00 taxes included.

You will receive one free Test Report Form (TRF) for yourself and we will send up to five free TRFs to the receiving institutions you indicated during your application.

Online Payment
Instructions will be given during the online application process. You may pay online for the test or for TRF courier services.

Telephone Payment
Contact the test centre by telephone to provide a credit card number after applying online: (416) 415-5000 x2564
Do not leave a voice mail with your card number. You may leave your telephone number and we will contact you.

In person Payment
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Debit, Certified Cheques or Money Orders in Person at the Test Centre. We do not accept cash or personal cheques.

You will receive your confirmation when we receive your complete application.



Refund and Cancellation Policy

Cancellation

For cancellations received no later than five weeks before the test date, the test fee will be refunded, less an administrative charge of 25% of your test fee. To cancel your test date, please email us at ielts@georgebrown.ca to receive the appropriate form.

Change of Test Date

You may change your test day once as long as you change it no later than 5 weeks before your originally scheduled date and the new test date is within 3 months of the original test date. To change your test date, please email us at ielts@georgebrown.ca to receive the appropriate form. There is a $25.00 fee to change your date.

Illness on the Test Day

If you are ill on the test day we need the following documents from you within five working days of the test date:
  • An original medical certificate covering the test date (only original sick notes are accepted)
  • A completed transfer/refund form (please email us for these.
If we have not received BOTH required documents by the fifth working day after the test, you will be marked as absent and forfeit all claims.

Please note that we do not accept notes issued by family members. Only notes signed by a certified medical doctor are acceptable.

If both documents reach us within the deadline, you have two options:
  • A full refund of the test fee, less an administrative charge of 25% of your test fee.
  • A single transfer to a new test date (within three months of the original test date)
Candidates who receive a transfer to a new test date and who are ill on the second test date are not entitled to a refund of the test fee or to any further transfer to a new test date.

There are no refunds or test-day transfers for candidates who do not bring their passport or national resident card with them on test day.
