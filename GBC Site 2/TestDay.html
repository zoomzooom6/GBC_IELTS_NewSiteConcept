Your IELTS Test Day Journey

PLEASE NOTE THAT ON TEST DAY, YOU WILL NEED TO BRING THE IDENTIFICATION DOCUMENT YOU REGISTERED WITH
Step 1: Arrive at your venue. Registration opens between 8 and 8:30AM.

Step 2: You may find it preferable to use the washroom now, prior to step 3.

Step 3: Leave all your personal belongings (including coats, hats, bags and purses, wallets) in the cloakroom. Cellphones and watches are not permitted in the testing room. Pens, pencils and erasers will be provided. You are permitted to bring a clear plastic water bottle with the label removed into the testing room.

Step 4: Check-in using your Passport or Permanent Resident Card. If you arrive at check-in after 9:20AM you will not be permitted to write the test. If you do not bring your identification you will not be permitted to write the test.

Step 5: Do a finger scan and have your photo taken.

Step 6: You will be escorted to the testing room.

Step 7: 9:30AM to 12:30PM: Listening, Reading, and Writing test.

Step 8: After the Listening, Reading and Writing test concludes, collect your belongings in the cloakroom. You should leave venue and to return for your speaking test.

Step 9: Return 20-40 minutes before your Speaking test for check-in and finger scan. Your Speaking test time is available online here.

Step 10: Test ends. Once your test has concluded, you will scan your finger upon exit and collect your belongings.
